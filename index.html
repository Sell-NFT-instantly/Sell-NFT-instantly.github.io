Questions

The three people always ask.

How can a sale be instant?
Instead of waiting for a buyer, the appraisal engine prices your NFT against traits, floor and live bids and stands behind a firm offer you can take immediately. Accepting settles the sale in seconds.
Do you take custody of my NFT?
No. It stays in your wallet until the moment you accept an offer. Placing an NFT to see its price never moves it and never puts it up for sale.
Which NFTs can I sell?
Anything with a real market — from blue-chip collections to long-tail pieces. If comparable sales and bids exist, the engine can quote it.
