How it works

From your wallet to paid, in one motion.

Selling normally is a project: list, wait, negotiate, re-price, pay gas. Sell NFT replaces all of it with a single appraise-and-accept motion. Here is exactly what happens.

The flow

Three moves, start to finish.

STEP 01

Place your NFT

Drop an image straight into the appraisal slot, or connect a wallet and select a piece. This is a read-only step — nothing is listed, transferred or exposed for sale.

STEP 02

The engine appraises it

In seconds it scans comparable sales, the collection floor, the item's traits and rarity, and live bids — then returns one firm, itemized offer with the reasoning shown.

STEP 03

Accept and get paid

One click accepts the offer. The swap settles almost instantly and funds land in your wallet. If you don't like the number, you simply walk away — nothing changes.

How the number is built

An instant price you can actually trust.

Instant doesn't mean arbitrary. Every offer is assembled from live, on-chain market signals — the same ones a careful seller would check, weighed in a fraction of a second.

Comparable sales

Recent sales of the same collection and similar traits anchor the fair-value estimate.

Floor & live bids

The current floor and standing collection bids set the real, right-now liquidity level.

Traits & rarity

Rare attributes and rank push the estimate above the floor where the market rewards them.

How it resolves to one number
Comparables + Floor & bids + Traits · rarity − Instant-liquidity spread = Your instant offer

The small spread between the fair-value estimate and the instant offer is what pays for taking the asset off your hands right now instead of waiting for a buyer — shown to you, never hidden.

Settlement & safety

Fast because it's simple — safe because you stay in control.

~9-second settlement

Acceptance triggers a single atomic swap — your NFT out, funds in — that confirms in seconds, not business days.

Non-custodial by design

Your NFT never leaves your wallet until the atomic swap executes. There is no deposit step and no intermediary holding your asset.

Walk away anytime

An offer is an option, not an obligation. Decline and nothing moves — no fee, no listing left behind, no trace.
