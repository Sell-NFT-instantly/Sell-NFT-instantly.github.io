About Sell NFT Instantly & Digital Collectibles

Sell NFT Instantly is a non-custodial portal for NFT trading, minting, and portfolio management.

What is an NFT?

Non-Fungible Tokens (NFTs) are unique digital identifiers registered on a blockchain ledger. Unlike fungible tokens, each NFT represents a distinct asset. Read more on the non-fungible token (NFT) Wikipedia page.

Decentralized NFT Trading

In a decentralized NFT market, buy and sell orders are settled directly via smart contracts. Users trade assets wallet-to-wallet, keeping ownership of their collectibles, reviewed on the OpenSea marketplace.

Verifiable Ownership

Because NFTs are stored on a public blockchain, their transaction history and current ownership are fully transparent, tracked on databases like the CoinMarketCap NFT stats directory.
